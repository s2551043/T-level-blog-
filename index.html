My T Level Journey: Digital Production, Design and Development

Hey there! I'm excited to share my journey into the world of T Levels in Digital Production, Design and Development. As someone completely new to this qualification, I'm both thrilled and a bit nervous about what's to come!

What are T Levels?

T Levels are a new 2-year qualification that combine classroom learning with a substantial industry placement. They're designed to give students the knowledge and experience needed to jump-start their careers in the digital industry.

What Will I Learn?

Over the next two years, I'll be diving deep into topics like:

  • Programming and coding
  • Data analysis and problem-solving
  • Digital security
  • Emerging technologies like IoT, AI, and AR

Industry Placement

One of the most exciting parts of this T Level is the 45-day industry placement. It's a chance to apply what we learn in the classroom to real-world scenarios. I can't wait to see what that experience will be like!

Future Prospects

After completing this T Level, I'll have options to enter the workforce directly, pursue higher education, or even start an apprenticeship. The digital skills I'll gain are in high demand, which is really encouraging for my future career prospects.
